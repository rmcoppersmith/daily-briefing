NASA's Artemis II Moon Mission Launch Window Opens Wednesday

After years of delays and more than $44 billion spent, the launch window for NASA's Artemis II mission opens Wednesday evening. If conditions are right, four astronauts will blast toward the moon, travel around it, and return to Earth in a roughly 10-day mission.
